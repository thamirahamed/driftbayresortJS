Welcome to

Driftbay Resort

Luxury Beach Resorts in Sri Lanka

Driftbay Resort sits at the edge of the beautiful, untouched coastline of southwest Sri Lanka. Located in Kalutara, our charming refuge offers private pool choices and a special mix of exciting adventure activities. Embark on a culinary adventure that celebrates the finest local ingredients and international flavors.

Whether you choose to feel the sun's gentle hug, explore the vibrant culture of Sri Lanka, or simply enjoy the comforts of your own private oasis, Driftbay Resort promises an experience that goes beyond your wildest dreams. Welcome to a retreat where dreams drift into reality.

Villas & Suites

Experience refined luxury in our suites with each suite offering spacious living area, plush furnishings, and breathtaking ocean views. Loosen up on your private balcony or relax in the cozy king-sized bed, while attentive service ensures you have a wonderful day.

Discover unmatched opulence at our villas which feature private pools, contemporary decor and stunning ocean panoramas. Enjoy chef-prepared meals, spa-like bathrooms and exclusive living, all amidst the beauty of the coast.

Dining

Experience a culinary journey from Asia to Italy at our resort. Discover the freshest seafood in diverse cuisines that celebrate vibrant colors and spices at our restaurants:

  • Spice Isle - Sri Lankan Cuisine
  • Piazza - Italian Cuisine
  • Hanamichi - Japanese Cuisine

The hotel enforces a rigorous policy concerning cleanliness, food hygiene, and freshness, all while creating an ideal dining atmosphere.

Our Spaces

At Driftbay, we take great pride in providing a wide variety of facilities designed to meet your needs and elevate your experience. We've got:

  1.   Spa
  2.   Fitness Centre
  3.   Swimming Pools
  4.   Beach Cabanas

Refresh your senses at our spa for holistic wellness, and stay active at out modern fitness center. Enjoy a leisurely evening at one of our beach cabanas. Driftbay Resort promises unforgettable moments amidst these exceptional facilities
